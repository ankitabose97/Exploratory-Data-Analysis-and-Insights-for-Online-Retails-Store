# Exploratory-Data-Analysis-and-Insights-for-Online-Retails-Store
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis using Pandas, Seaborn and Matplotlib library to clean and leverage consumer data for an Online Retails Store to identify trends, improve  customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies.


## Goals of the Project:
  A leading retail company wants to better understand its customers’ shopping behavior to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.
  The management team has noticed changes in purchasing patterns across demographics, product categories, and sales channels (online vs. offline). 
  They are particularly interested in uncovering which factors, such as discounts, reviews, seasons, or payment preferences, drive consumer decisions and repeat     purchases. 
  
  As a Data Analyst, my role is  to analyze the company’s consumer behavior dataset to leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer          engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies.
  
## Outline of the Project:
  - i) Libraries import
  - ii) Dataset exploration and Cleaning
  - iii) Groupby tables
  - iv) Visualization in Pandas  - Use Matplotlib and Seaborn to create insightful visualizations to uncover insights
  - v) Analyse data in SQL to answer business questions 

## Questions:

In this project, I tried to give answers to a set of questions that may be relevant when analyzing Sales data:

 - What is the expenditure pattern of different genders?
 - What is the expenditure pattern of different age groups?
 - What are the top 10 items in terms of revenue?
 - What are the top-performing categories, and are ongoing discount strategies boosting sales?
 - What are the most preferred payment processes and shipping types?
 - Are there any seasonal trends?
 - In addition, I made a visual analysis to plan marketing campaigns more effectively.

## Insights:
 - Core Demographics: The primary customer base is heavily skewed toward Male buyers, with the Gen Z cohort leading total purchase volume.

 - Product Performance: Customer demand is highly concentrated in the Clothing and Accessories categories, with a clear concentration of sales among the Top 10 most popular items across the board.

  - Discounting & Revenue: Discount strategies are currently causing revenue leakage; data shows that the vast majority of transactions are successfully happening at full price (without any discount).

 - Fulfillment Preferences: Customers prefer transactional flexibility, leaning on PayPal, Cash, and Credit Cards for payments, and prioritizing Free, Express, and Standard Shipping.

 - Seasonality: Sales are uniform throughout the year, showing a minor peak during the Spring Season.

## Optimized Product & Marketing Strategy: 
 - Hyper-Target Gen Z Males: Launch aggressive social media marketing campaigns (e.g., via TikTok, Instagram, or gaming platforms) specifically featuring male apparel and accessories to capture this highly active audience.

 - Capitalize on the Top 10: Feature the Top 10 best-selling items prominently on your homepage, in "Trending Now" email blasts, and as recommended add-ons at checkout to boost average order value.

 - Spring-Loaded Campaigns: Allocate the bulk of your yearly marketing budget to Q1/early Q2 to ride the Spring sales wave, introducing fresh, seasonal capsule collections in Clothing and Accessories right as demand spikes.

 - Streamline Checkout Perks: Keep payment friction zero by highlighting PayPal and Credit Card logos at checkout, and use "Free Shipping" as an explicit incentive banner across the top of your website.

## Improvements for Non-Performing Areas
 - Plug the Discount Leakage: Tighten your promotional strategies immediately. Reduce sitewide or blanket discounts since customers are already willing to buy at full retail price. Reserve discounts only for clearing out slow-moving inventory or as exclusive rewards for high-tier loyal customers.

 - Revive Underperforming Categories: For product categories lagging behind Clothing and Accessories, run bundled promotions (e.g., "Buy a Top-10 Jacket, get a Home/Lifestyle item at 30% off") to expose blind spots in your inventory.

 - Optimize Off-Season Slumps: To fix the post-Spring revenue drop, implement mid-year product drops, flash sales, or exclusive pre-orders to sustain engagement and steady cash flow during slower seasons.
